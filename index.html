The Flock

Homemade on Duval Street since day one.

We are a small tropical ice cream shop on the sunnier end of Duval, run by a flock of Key West lifers. Every batch is churned in the back of the store — no wholesale mixes, no artificial flavors, and no shortcuts on the good stuff. If a flavor calls for guava, we peel guava.

The menu leans into what actually grows in the Keys and the Caribbean — key limes, mamey, guava, and whatever tropical fruit came off the boat that morning. Regulars call themselves the flock. New here? Just walk in — the flamingo sign is hard to miss.

Best of the Island

Seven Bubba's, in a row.

The Bubba's are Key West's locals'-choice awards — an annual popular vote where residents crown the island's best in every category from breakfast to buskers.

For seven years running, Flamingo Crossing has taken home Best Ice Cream / Gelato.
